## Getting Started

### Registration
1. Visit flynjet.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your details
4. Verify your email
5. Complete your profile

### Login

- Social login options

### Step-by-Step Booking
1. Go to Bookings page
2. Enter flight details:

   - Date

3. Select aircraft
4. Review pricing
5. Add passengers
6. Make payment
7. Receive confirmation
